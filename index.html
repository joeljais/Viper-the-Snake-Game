Privacy & Policy

Data Storage: All game data is stored locally on your device using localStorage. No data is transmitted to any server.

Account Security: Passwords are hashed before storage. However, since data is stored locally, treat this as a casual game — do not use sensitive passwords.

Chat: World chat messages are stored locally and visible only to you. In a production version, messages would be moderated.

Cookies: This game does not use cookies or tracking.

Third Parties: No third-party services collect your data.

Data Deletion: Use "Reset All Data" in Settings to delete all stored data.
